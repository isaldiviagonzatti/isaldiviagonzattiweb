| sit and look out upon all the sorrows of the world, and upon
all oppression and shame

| hear secret convulsive sobs from young men at anguish
with themselves, remorseful after deeds done,

| see in low life the mother misused by her children, dying,
neglected, gaunt,desperate

| see the wife misused by her husband, | see the treacherous
seducer of young women

| mark the ranklings of jealousy and unrequited love
attempted to be hid, | see these sights on the earth

| see the workings of battle,pestilence,tyranny, | see martyrs
and prisoners,
